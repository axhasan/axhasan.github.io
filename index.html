Musings
March 8, 2026
Watching the AI spending wave with fascination — $320B in a single year, and yet the labour market is only now beginning to feel it. The cuts are real, but they lag the investment cycle by 18–24 months. By late 2026, I expect we'll see the inflection: productivity data that finally matches the capex numbers. The question isn't whether AI replaces jobs — it's whether the new jobs it creates pay as well. So far, the evidence is mixed.
